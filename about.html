About

Meg Klepacz is a writer and illustrator. She enjoys writing about the vast, complex, and sometimes disturbing inner lives of women. Her illustrations range from the absurd to introspective mini comics. A native born Ohioan, Meg has lived in the far north, central, and now southwestern reaches of that great state, but home is mostly wherever her bird is. She is currently writing her first novel. Her short stories and illustrations can be found on her Substack page.
